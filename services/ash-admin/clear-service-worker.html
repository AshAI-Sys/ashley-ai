This tool will help you clear the service worker and its cache that's causing the fetch errors.

What's causing the errors?

The service worker is trying to cache Next.js development files that change frequently. This has been fixed in the code, but your browser still has the old service worker active.

Manual Steps (if needed):

  1. Open Browser DevTools (F12)
  2. Go to Application tab
  3. Click Service Workers on the left
  4. Click Unregister for localhost:3001
  5. Click Storage on the left
  6. Click Clear site data
  7. Reload the page (Ctrl+Shift+R)
